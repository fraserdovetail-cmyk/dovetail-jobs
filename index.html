How to use this

  1. Fill in the form above with as much detail as you have about the job.
  2. Click "Generate & save" — the prompt is copied to clipboard and saved against this job in Google Sheets.
  3. Open a new Claude chat at claude.ai.
  4. Upload the blank Excel template (Surname_-_scope_of_work.xlsx) using the paperclip icon.
  5. Paste the prompt and send. Claude will populate all four tabs of the workbook.
  6. Review the assumptions list at the end of Claude's response before sending to the client.
